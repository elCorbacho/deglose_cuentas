# Design System Theme

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional properties of our design system, ensuring consistency and a cohesive user experience across all products and platforms.

## Color Palette

Our color palette is designed to be vibrant and accessible, reflecting the brand's identity while prioritizing user experience.

- **Color Mode**: `light`
- **Primary Color**: `#0056D2` - This is the brand's most distinctive chromatic color, ideal for call-to-action buttons, key interactive elements, and primary branding.
- **Secondary Color**: `#50E3C2` - A supporting color used for less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions, providing a balanced contrast.
- **Tertiary Color**: `#9B51E0` - An additional accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements, adding visual interest.
- **Neutral Color**: `#F8FAFC` - This neutral base color is used for backgrounds, surfaces, and non-chromatic elements, providing a clean and consistent foundation.

## Typography

We utilize a carefully selected set of fonts to ensure readability and establish a clear visual hierarchy.

- **Headline Font**: `plusJakartaSans` - Used for titles and headings, providing strong visual impact.
- **Body Font**: `inter` - Chosen for its legibility and versatility in various text sizes for body content.
- **Label Font**: `inter` - Applied to labels and smaller text elements, maintaining consistency with body text for optimal readability.

## Shape and Spacing

These properties define the physical characteristics of our UI components, influencing the overall feel and interaction.

- **Roundedness**: `2` (Moderate) - Corners of UI shapes feature a moderate degree of roundedness, striking a balance between modern and friendly aesthetics.
- **Spacing**: `2` (Normal) - A normal level of whitespace is maintained within and between UI elements, ensuring clarity without feeling overly dense or sparse.
